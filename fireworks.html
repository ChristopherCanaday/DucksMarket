Fireworks

Must be 16 or older to purchase!

      Here at Duck's Market, we have a wide variety of fireworks from .35¢ firecrackers to $395 assortments. We sell year-round. Our main supplier is Black Cat®, but we also get fireworks from Brothers and Worldclass.

History of Black Cat®

      Black Cat® started in the U.S. during the 1940’s and registered with the U.S. Patent & Trademark Office in 1952. Black Cat® is the oldest registered consumer fireworks brand in the U.S., and is one of the most recognized fireworks brands. From 1952 to the present, Black Cat® remains “The Best You Can Get” in consumer fireworks!
